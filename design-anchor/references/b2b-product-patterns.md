# B2B Product Patterns

Read this before generating a new B2B product, admin system, dashboard, CRM, ERP, internal tool, approval flow, settings area, analytics page, monitoring console, or data-management page.

## Product Frame

Identify the product frame before choosing visual style:

- Roles: admin, operator, manager, reviewer, customer success, finance, developer, analyst, or end customer.
- Core objects: customers, orders, projects, tickets, invoices, tasks, alerts, assets, policies, reports, workflows, or integrations.
- Workflow: browse, filter, edit, approve, monitor, configure, investigate, report, onboard, resolve, or export.
- Density: compact workbench, balanced SaaS, trust-heavy business, friendly platform, dark focused workspace, or command center.

## Common Page Types

- Dashboard: KPI cards, trend charts, alerts, recent activity, ownership, and quick actions.
- List management: filters, search, table, saved views, bulk actions, row states, and pagination.
- Detail page: summary header, status, metadata, tabs, timeline, related records, and primary actions.
- Create/edit form: grouped fields, validation, sticky actions, permissions, and preview when useful.
- Approval queue: priority, assignee, SLA, decision actions, comments, evidence, and audit trail.
- Settings: sections, toggles, permissions, integration status, defaults, and danger zone.
- Analytics/reporting: date ranges, segments, comparison, drill-down, exports, and annotations.
- Monitoring console: live status, severity, ownership, incidents, logs, and escalation path.
- Permission management: roles, scopes, members, inherited access, exceptions, and audit log.

## B2B UI Rules

Prioritize scanability, comparison, repeated action, clear state, and reversible operations. Avoid marketing-page composition unless the user explicitly asks for a landing page.

Use complete workflows instead of isolated widgets. A good B2B screen should show what the user can see, decide, and do next.

## Preset Hints

- Choose `linear` for dense workbenches, operations consoles, task systems, and data-heavy admin pages.
- Choose `stripe` for billing, finance, trust-heavy portals, account management, and compliance workflows.
- Choose `saas-style-01` for modern SaaS dashboards, onboarding, analytics, and product-led collaboration.
- Choose `google-style` for friendly settings, education, support, CRM, and broad internal tools.
- Choose `minimal-dark` for AI workspaces, developer tools, and focused premium productivity.
- Choose `hud-dark-style` for security monitoring, risk control, trading, live ops, and command centers.
